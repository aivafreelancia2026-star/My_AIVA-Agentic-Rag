📖 How to Use Catapult Bot

🚀 Getting Started

Catapult Bot is your intelligent document analysis assistant. Here's how to make the most of it:

🔍 Search Modes

  • General + Docs (Default): The bot can answer both general questions and questions about your documents
  • Only Documents: The bot will only answer based on your loaded documents

📚 Working with Documents

  • All accessible documents are automatically loaded for you
  • Click on the Documents section to view and manage your document selection
  • Only documents you have permission to access will be shown
  • Selected documents will be highlighted

💬 Asking Questions

  • Type your question in the chat box at the bottom
  • Press Enter or click the send button
  • Ask follow-up questions - the bot remembers context!
  • Use the Stop button to halt response generation

✨ Key Features

  • Role-Based Access: Secure document access based on your designation
  • Table Analysis: Extract and analyze data from tables
  • Image Processing: OCR and content analysis from images
  • Context Awareness: Maintains conversation history
  • Real-time Streaming: See responses as they're generated
